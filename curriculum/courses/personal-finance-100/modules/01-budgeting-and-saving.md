# Budgeting & Saving

Income vs. expenses, emergency funds, and the power of compound interest.

## Learning objectives
- Distinguish income from expenses and compute a simple monthly budget.
- Explain the purpose of an emergency fund.
- Demonstrate compound interest over time with a slider/scrubber simulation.

## Check figures
- A $100/month deposit at 5% APY for 10 years grows to ≈ $15,528.
