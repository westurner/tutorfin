# Common Core topic codes

Per-subject mapping of Common Core State Standards (math) most relevant to each course. These are starting points; refine per lesson.

## Personal Finance 100
- **Budgeting & Saving**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.RP.A.3.C — Find a percent of a quantity; solve problems involving finding the whole.
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Use proportional relationships to solve multistep ratio and percent problems (interest).
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSF.IF.C.8.B — Use properties of exponents (compound growth/decay).
- **Credit & Debt**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Percent problems including simple interest.
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSF.LE.A.2 — Construct linear and exponential functions (amortization).
- **Paying for College**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.SP.B.4 — Compare numerical data distributions (cost vs. earnings).
- **Investing Basics**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSS.MD.B.5 — Evaluate outcomes of decisions involving payoffs (risk/return).
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSF.IF.C.8.B — Exponential growth (compounding).
- **Taxes & Insurance**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Percent (tax brackets, withholding).
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSS.MD.B.5 — Expected value (insurance pooling).

## Project Accounting 100
- **Project Budgeting**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.EE.B.7 — Solve real-world problems by writing equations (budgets).
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Percent (variance vs. budget).
- **Direct vs. Indirect Costs**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.RP.A.3 — Ratio reasoning (overhead allocation drivers).
- **Tracking & Ledger Basics**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.NS.A.1 — Add/subtract rational numbers (debits/credits as signed amounts).
- **Project Profitability**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Percent (margin, ROI).

## Business Finance 100
- **The Accounting Equation**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.EE.B.5 — Solve equations (Assets = Liabilities + Equity).
- **Financial Statements**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.SP.B.5 — Summarize numerical data (statement totals).
- **Unit Economics**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.7.RP.A.3 — Percent (gross margin per unit).
- **Capital & Funding**
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.HSS.MD.B.5 — Decisions under risk (debt vs. equity).
